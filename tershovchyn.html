RUBY RB1 CHOCOLATE

THE 4TH CHOCOLATE IS HERE, BORN FROM THE RUBY COCOA BEAN

After dark, milk and white, ruby is the most uncommon chocolate discovery in 80 years. This gift of mother nature surprises and bedazzles with a completely new colour and taste experience, born from the ruby cocoa bean - without adding any colourants or fruit flavourings.

With the Finest Belgian Chocolate ruby RB1, Callebaut brings the first ever expression of ruby chocolate fully dedicated to artisans and chefs. Immerse yourself in its ruby colour. Lose yourself in its intense fruitiness and fresh, sour notes. Let ruby RB1 be the spark to ignite fresh creativity.
